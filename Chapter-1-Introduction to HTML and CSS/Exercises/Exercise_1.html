Butter Chicken
Recipe


This is a creamy Indian dish featuring tender chicken in a rich tomato-based sauce.



  • 2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons ground turmeric
  • 2 teaspoons garam masala
  • 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 tablespoon minced ginger
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish



    • Method

    1. In a bowl, mix together yogurt, lemon juice, turmeric, garam masala, cumin, paprika, ginger, and garlic.
    2. Add chicken to the marinade, cover, and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, preferably overnight.
    3. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
    4. Place marinated chicken on a baking sheet and bake for 20-25 minutes or until cooked through.
    5. In a large skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Add tomato puree and cook for 5 minutes.
    6. Add heavy cream and simmer for another 5 minutes.
    7. Stir in baked chicken and cook for an additional 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
    8. Season with salt to taste and garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.
